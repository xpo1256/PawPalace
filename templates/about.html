About PawPalace

Connecting loving families with adorable dogs since 2024. We're passionate about helping every dog find their perfect forever home.

Our Mission

At PawPalace, we believe every dog deserves a loving home and every family deserves the joy of a furry companion. Our mission is to create a safe, trustworthy platform where responsible sellers can connect with caring buyers.

We're committed to promoting responsible pet ownership, supporting animal welfare, and ensuring that every transaction on our platform leads to a lifetime of happiness for both dogs and their new families.

Our Values

These core values guide everything we do at PawPalace

Love & Compassion

Every dog deserves love, care, and respect. We promote compassionate treatment and responsible ownership in all our interactions.
